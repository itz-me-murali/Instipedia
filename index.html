Overview

About IIT Madras

Established in 1959 with German technical assistance, IITM is consistently India's #1 ranked institution — and one of Asia's most beautiful campuses.

🏆 Rankings (2025–26)

  • #1 NIRF Overall — 7th consecutive year
  • #1 NIRF Engineering — since 2016
  • #1 NIRF Innovation 2025
  • #2 NIRF Research Institutions 2025
  • #180 QS World University Rankings 2026
  • #31 QS Global Engineering & Technology 2026

📍 Location & Gates

  • 617-acre campus carved from Guindy National Park
  • ~10 km from Chennai Airport, ~12 km from Chennai Central
  • Main Gate — Sardar Patel Road
  • Velachery Gate — pedestrians & two-wheelers
  • Taramani Gate — pedestrians & two-wheelers

🏫 Campus at a Glance

  • 16 academic departments, 100+ labs
  • 674 faculty, 10,000+ students (UG, PG, and PhD)
  • 233+ startups via IITM Incubation Cell
  • IITM Research Park connected via Industry-Academic Bridge
  • Discovery Campus at Thaiyur — 163 acres, 36 km away

🐾 Campus Wildlife

  • Part of protected forest from Guindy National Park
  • Home to Blackbuck — India's protected antelope
  • Chital (spotted deer), bonnet macaques, peacocks, snakes
  • Prakriti Wildlife Club runs guided nature walks

🎓 New Programmes 2024–25

  • BTech in Computational Engineering & Mechanics (CEM)
  • BTech in Instrumentation & Biomedical Engineering (iBME)
  • MTech in Electric Vehicles
  • Sports Excellence Admission (SEA) — first IIT to do so
  • Fine Arts Admission (FACE)

🎯 Mission

  • Create a community of learning for professional & ethical knowledge application
  • Pursue and disseminate research findings
  • Provide knowledge-based services to society and industry
  • Build national capabilities in Science, Technology, Humanities & Management

Campus

Major Landmarks

Gajendra Circle (GC) is your north star. All campus directions are described relative to it.

⭕

Gajendra Circle (GC)

Heart of campus. All roads, bus routes and directions are described relative to GC. Great spot to hang out at night.

📚

Central Library

Five-storeyed near GC. Ajanta reading hall open 24/7. Has a book bank for semester-long textbook borrowing.

🎭

Open Air Theatre (OAT)

Amphitheatre between GC and the hostel area. Film Club screens movies every Saturday night. Saarang shows held here.

🔬

Centre For Innovation (CFI)

24/7 student laboratory near the academic complex. Home to robotics, aero, coding, and all technical teams.

🏛️

Students' Activity Centre (SAC)

Registration venue, music room, large auditorium. Houses 4 badminton courts (wooden floor). Institute Day and Convocation held here.

💼

Career Pathway Centre

Career Development Cell, Entrepreneurship Cell, Placement & Internship Office — all under one roof.

🏥

Institute Hospital & Wellness

Primary health care on First Cross Road. Wellness Centre (DoST Building, 1st floor) for mental health consultations.

🚀

IITM Research Park

India's first university-based research park. Connected to campus via Industry-Academic Bridge. 233+ startups incubated here.

🏢

NAC-I & NAC-II

New Academic Complex I near Campus Café. NAC-II adjacent to Ocean Engineering Department and opposite Biotech-II. State-of-the-art lecture halls and labs.

📡

Central Lecture Theatre (CLT)

Air-conditioned hall in Humanities & Sciences block. Used for quizzes, special lectures, and cultural events.

🏢

Administration Block

Near GC. Academic, Accounts, Engineering Unit, Security, and Heritage Centre all here. Heritage Centre open to visitors.

🎪

IC&SR Building

Opposite HSS on Alumni Avenue. Auditorium has hosted Dr. Kiran Bedi, Kamal Haasan, and many distinguished personalities.

🌿

Ellora

Hangout spot next to DoMS building. Popular for chilling between classes and study breaks.

🛍️

Quark Student Space

Near Saraswathi hostel. Restaurant on ground floor. 81 Lounge for student activities on 1st floor.

🧘

UTIL & DoST

Up-Top Interaction Lounge on roof of DoST Building. Wellness Centre for mental health also in DoST premises, 1st floor.

🏨

Guest Houses

Bose-Einstein Guest House (BEGH) for VIPs and institute guests. Taramani Guest House (TGH) for guests, faculty, and students.

🔧

Central Skill Training & Fabrication Facility (CSTF)

Formerly the Central Workshop, established in 1959. Trains students in foundry, welding, CNC machines, electronics, carpentry, fitting and more. ISO 9001 certified. Open to all BTech students for hands-on manufacturing skills.

Events

Major Fests

IITM's fests are among India's biggest student-run events. As a fresher, these will be your first taste of Insti life.

Saarang
Annual Cultural Festival
India's second oldest college cultural festival, founded in 1974. Five days of music, dance, drama, arts, FMX shows, celebrity performances, flea market. Saarang 2025 (51st edition) featured Amit Trivedi and Ritviz.
80,000+ footfall January annually 5 days Since 1974
Shaastra
Annual Technical Festival
One of India's largest student-run tech festivals, ISO 9001:2015 certified. Robot wars, AI workshops, rover challenges, drone events, armed forces exhibitions. Shaastra 2026 (27th edition) theme was "Artifacts of Arcade".
80,000+ visitors January annually 80 events 750+ organisers
E-Summit
Entrepreneurship Summit
Flagship event by E-Cell IITM. Founders, investors, startup showcases, pitch competitions, and panel discussions with India's top entrepreneurs. ISO, UNESCO, and Startup India certified — the first student-run entrepreneurial gathering in India to receive all three.
15,000+ attendees Feb–Mar annually 1,000+ founders 400+ colleges
Schroeter & Inter-Hostel Events
All Year Long
Inter-hostel sports, cultural battles, and academic competitions throughout the year. First chances to represent your hostel and bond with batchmates. IITM won the 2025 General Championship at Inter-IIT Sports Meet.

Governance

Elections at IIT Madras

Annual elections let students shape their own campus. Every vote matters.

🏛️ Students' Legislative Council (SLC)

  • Primary legislative body of the student community
  • Addresses concerns and collaborates with administration

🏠 Hostel Affairs Council (HAC)

  • Accommodation, dining, and hostel welfare matters
  • Works with Office of Hostel Management (OHM)

📖 Academic Affairs Council (AAC)

  • Curriculum feedback and academic policy improvements
  • Works with faculty to enhance academic environment

🎪 Co-curricular Committees

  • Oversee cultural, technical, sports, and literary clubs
  • Coordinate inter-hostel and inter-collegiate events

How Elections Work

1

Eligibility

All registered students can vote. Candidates must have minimum academic standing and no disciplinary actions.

2

Nomination

File nominations with peer endorsements and a manifesto outlining your goals for the term.

3

Campaigning

Debates, posters, and social media within institute guidelines. Digital campaigning has grown significantly.

4

Voting

Secret ballots or secure electronic voting to ensure confidentiality and integrity.

5

Results & Term

Votes tallied transparently. Elected officials serve for one full academic year.

Getting Around

Transport & Connectivity

🚌 City Buses

  • Madhya Kailash and CLRI/Cancer Institute — near Main Gate
  • Gandhi Mandapam and Guru Nanak College — near Velachery Gate
  • CMBT Koyambedu for outstation buses
  • Check MTC website for route numbers

🚇 Metro & MRTS

  • Kasturba Nagar MRTS Station — 1.2 km from Main Gate
  • Little Mount Metro — ~3 km, 5–10 min by road
  • Guindy Metro — ~3.3 km, 5–10 min by road

🚂 Trains

  • Guindy local train station — 3.3 km from campus
  • Outstation trains from Chennai Central (~12 km) and Egmore
  • Railway ticket booking at the campus Post Office (limited schedule)

🚍 On-Campus Bus

  • Runs 6:15 AM – 9:30 PM every 15 minutes
  • Last bus: 10:00 PM (Main Gate → Bus Depot via GC)
  • Late-night on-call bus: 9:30 PM – 4:00 AM
  • Call Security: 044-2257 9999

🛺 Autos & Cabs

  • Ola, Uber, Rapido all work well around IITM
  • Auto stands near Main Gate, Velachery Gate, Taramani Gate
  • Negotiate fares for autos; metered cabs more reliable

🚲 Cycling on Campus

  • Cycling is practical and popular on the 617-acre campus
  • Speed Buy Cycle Repair near Sangam Ground
  • Many students buy cycles — highly recommended for freshers
